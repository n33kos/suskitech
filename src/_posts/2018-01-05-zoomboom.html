  • Up: Move Up
  • Left: Move Left
  • Right: Move Right
  • Down: Move Down

ZoomBoom is a pico-8 aerial avoidance game coded in lua for Pico-8. The objective is to avoid being struck with missles, while passing through the objective rings.
