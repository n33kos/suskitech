    <h1>About</h1>
    <p>The objective is to avoid being struck with missles, while passing through the objective rings.</p>

    <h1>Controls</h1>
    <ul>
      <li>z: Speed Boost</li>
      <li>x: Speed Boost</li>
      <li>Up: Move Up</li>
      <li>Left: Move Left</li>
      <li>Right: Move Right</li>
      <li>Down: Move Down</li>
